This website was created to document my love of amusement parks and roller coasters. Last updated in October 2020.


About Erin:

I have considered myself a coaster enthusiast since 2006, when I rode Rock 'n' Roller Coaster on a family trip to Walt Disney World. My dad also enjoys coasters a lot, and I credit him with both convincing me to ride "big" coasters at a young age and encouraging me to start this website. Currently, I have ridden 153 different roller coasters in the USA, Canada, and England! I hold many unpopular opinions when compared to the general consensus of the enthusiast community, as I value speed, inversions, smoothness, and airtime (preferably floater) most when I rank and review rides. Outside of coasters, I have a B.S. Mathematics (2019) and I have worked as a private math tutor since graduation. I love my two adorable dogs, mystery books, jigsaw puzzles and playing the euphonium.
